Good questions, answered.

What exactly is Herae?

Herae is an app that lets you watch anything together, in perfect sync, on almost any website — with voice, video, and chat built right in. It's made for long-distance couples, friends, and family who want a real night in from anywhere.

How is it different from screen sharing?

Screen sharing streams a laggy, blurry copy of your screen and drains your battery. Herae keeps everyone on their own crisp, full-quality video and just syncs the play, pause, and skip — so it looks perfect for everyone at once, and you can actually talk over it.

Does it really work on any website?

Almost any. Unlike watch-party tools locked to one or two streaming services, Herae syncs video across nearly any site — including embeds most other tools can't reach. Wherever the video already is, Herae works there.

Is it free?

Yes — Herae is free to start. Add it to Chrome, create an account in seconds, and start watching together.

What are Herae Moments?

Herae Moments is your shared, private timeline. After every session you can capture photos, save reaction videos, review what you watched, name the night, and like or comment on it later — so your movie nights add up into a scrapbook of your relationship you can relive any time, for years.

Is it private?

Yes. Once you're connected, your video and voice calls are peer-to-peer — they go straight between you and never pass through a server. And every memory you save is private by default — just for the two of you.

What do I need to get started?

Just Chrome on a computer, and someone to watch with. Add the free extension, sign in, and open the same page together — that's it.
